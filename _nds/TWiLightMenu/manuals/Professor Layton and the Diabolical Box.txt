***********************
Using the Hamster Mode:
***********************
The main objective of the
Hamster mode is to make the fat
hamster into a slim hamster. Do
this by placing toys on his grid
and making him walk around.

The hamster starts with a ひまんど
(or "fatness level") of 5. It
says in the upper right corner
of the top screen. You must make
this 0 in order to make the
hamster slim.

On the left of the bottom
screen, is two numbers, both of
these count steps. The top
number (goal) is the target
number of steps. For example,
when you first start, this
number is 8, so 8 steps are
required to reduce the fatness
of the hamster by one. As the
hamster gets slimmer the target
number of steps increase. The
bottom number (record) is the
maximum number of steps you have
made the hamster walk in one go.

To make the hamster walk, you
put items on he'll walk to them.
He will see any items within 3
steps. Press the button at the
bottom to make him walk!

First a bit about the items:
  Apple - These are the hamster'
range, he'll go for them first.
  Block - These block the way of
  used strategically.
  Pet House - Nothing special.
  Flower - Nothing special.

  Surprise Box - This surprises 
    came from.
  Lightbulb - This blinds the ha
   facing.

  Sandbox  - Once he plays in th
  Pool - After playing in the sa
  'rolling' doesn't count as ste

  Trampoline - This allows the h

This isn't too hard, you only
need 30 steps to complete the
entire sidequest. You can
complete this sidequest during
chapter 4 if you try hard
enough. Anything after that is
just your own record.
